About

Billy D. Willz is a New York-based artist crafting cloud rap and cinematic atmospheric hip-hop. Influenced by Bob Marley, Stevie Wonder, Rap, Rock, West Indies, Dancehall, Latin music — the sound lives in the space between depth and light.
